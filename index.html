Al Qadi International

With nearly a decade of experience, Al Qadi International proudly serves as a globally-oriented food supply company based in Qatar. Since our establishment in 2013, we have diligently cultivated a reputation as a trusted distributor of premium-quality food products. Our extensive network spans renowned hypermarkets such as LULU, SAFARI, GRAND, INDIAN SUPERMARKET, NESTO, among others, showcasing our steadfast commitment to delivering excellence.
